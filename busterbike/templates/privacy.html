Privacy Policy for Buster Bike

Last Updated: 25-07-2024

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you sign up for Buster Bike, we collect your username and password.

Location Information: While using our app, we collect your location data to be able to locate the bike at the end of your ride.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information in various ways, including:

  • To locate the bike at the end of a ride
  • To maintain and improve our service

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties.

You have the right to delete your account at any time. However, please note that the records of rides you have made will not be deleted.
